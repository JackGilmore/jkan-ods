URL statuses by Council¶

This shows if the URL of OD assets are active (True) or not (False). If it is True, the data is accessible, if it is False there is a hosting issue. (Error) indicates there is no URL to check or it is missing from our data.

A full listing of the URL status is available in 'output_AllAnalytics.csv'
